About

I am a post-doctoral Research Associate at the Computational and Biological Learning Lab (CBL), Department of Engineering at University of Cambridge. My current research focuses on understanding how humans learn and adapt to pain and relief, by modelling brain imaging and physiological data with learning algorithms, supervised by Dr Ben Seymour. I am also interested in developing brain-computer interface and neurofeedback systems for chronic pain treatment.
